Plain-language quick add

Type "Pay rent Friday 9am #home !p1" and Beck parses the date, list, and priority for you — no menus.

Automation engine

Build if-this-then-that rules that route, tag, reschedule, and organize tasks automatically as things change.

Six ways to see your work

Today, Upcoming, Board, Calendar, Timeline, and an Eisenhower matrix — switch views without switching apps.

Time-blocking & auto-schedule

Drop tasks onto your calendar, or let Beck auto-schedule them into the gaps in your day.

Attachments

Advanced reminders

Time-Sensitive alerts, recurring reminders, and location reminders that fire when you arrive or leave a place.
